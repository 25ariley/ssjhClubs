Description

Student to Student is a student-led organization from the Military Child Education Coalition Initiative intended to create a more welcoming school environment for all students. Students in the club will be assigned duties to welcome new students, such as giving tours, or forming a group at lunch for new students to sit with. The noncurricular club focuses on acadmeics, service, and helping a new student transition to a new school.
